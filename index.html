About Me

I am an aspiring software developer from Austin, TX. I've lived in Austin for a little over a year and have lived in Texas most of my life. I've attended the University of Texas Pan American, Rutgers University, and graduated July 2017 from Texas State University with a Bachelor's of Art in History.
